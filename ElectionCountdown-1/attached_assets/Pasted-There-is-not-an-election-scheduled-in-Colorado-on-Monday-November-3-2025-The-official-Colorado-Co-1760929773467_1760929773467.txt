There is not an election scheduled in Colorado on Monday, November 3, 2025. The official Colorado Coordinated Election is set for Tuesday, November 4, 2025, which is the day most local and statewide ballot measures and races will be held throughout the state. The date shown in your image (November 3, 2025) is one day prior to the official election day, which follows the national tradition of elections being held on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in November.[1][2][3][4]
