You’re right to flag this. A **“Louisiana – federal special” on Fri, Aug 15, 2025** cannot be correct.

### What’s true

* **Louisiana holds its elections on *Saturdays*** by law. R.S. **18:402** sets the dates for primary/general elections (e.g., the 1st/last/5th **Saturday** patterns). ([Findlaw][1])
* **Special elections must be scheduled “in accordance with R.S. 18:402.”** That’s in **R.S. 18:591** (vacancies), which means specials also fall on the Saturday schedule—**not** on a Friday. ([Findlaw][2])

So a card that says **“Fri, Aug 15, 2025 – LA – federal special”** is invalid on two counts: the **day-of-week** and the **classification** (“federal special”) without a gubernatorial proclamation.
